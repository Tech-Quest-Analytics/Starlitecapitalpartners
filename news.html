Starlite Capital Partners Raises $700M+ to Expand AI and Enterprise Software Infrastructure

Starlite Capital Partners, a leading private investment firm specializing in private credit and equity solutions, announced today that it has successfully raised over $700 million in strategic capital to accelerate the expansion of its AI and enterprise software integration infrastructure.

This capital raise represents a major milestone in the firm's mission to provide forward-thinking financing solutions and drive long-term value creation for portfolio companies and institutional investors. The funds will be deployed to scale AI-driven enterprise systems, enhance technology infrastructure, and support strategic investments across high-growth sectors.

Our focus has always been on bridging opportunity with innovation. Raising over $700 million is more than just capital — it's the momentum to build the next generation of AI and enterprise software infrastructure. This allows us to strengthen our partnerships and deliver smarter, technology-driven solutions across industries.

Starlite Capital Partners leverages data-driven insights, disciplined asset management, and proprietary analytics tools to identify underutilized opportunities in private credit, equity, and technology integration. The new capital will also enable the firm to expand its geographic footprint and invest in sectors demonstrating strong performance and long-term resilience, including logistics, technology, and infrastructure.

"Investors are seeking stability, innovation, and scalable technology solutions," the spokesperson added. "We're building a firm that embodies all three, combining institutional-grade strategy with forward-thinking agility."

Founded to redefine private investment, Starlite Capital Partners continues to align financial strength with strategic foresight, ensuring its partners and portfolio companies thrive in a rapidly evolving global economy.
