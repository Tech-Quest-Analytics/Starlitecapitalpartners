Introduction

Welcome to Starlite Capital Partners ("Starlite," "we," "our," or "us"). We value your trust and are committed to protecting your personal and business information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and share your information when you visit our website, engage with our services, or interact with us online or offline.

By accessing or using our website and services, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our site and services.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • A. Information You Provide: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address, company name, job title, financial data, investment interests, payment details, and communications you send to us.
  • B. Information Automatically Collected: IP address, location, browser type, device, operating system, pages visited, and time spent on each page.
  • C. Information from Third Parties: Data from affiliates, partners, public databases, and compliance verification services for due diligence and legal compliance.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide and manage our investment and advisory services
  • Verify identity and perform due diligence
  • Process financial transactions and contractual obligations
  • Respond to requests and provide client support
  • Send updates, reports, and communications
  • Improve website performance and user experience
  • Comply with legal, regulatory, and anti-fraud obligations

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your information. We may share it only as follows:

  • Service Providers: Vendors assisting with hosting, analytics, and compliance.
  • Affiliates: Within the Starlite network for operations and compliance.
  • Legal Reasons: To comply with laws, subpoenas, or court orders.
  • Business Transactions: During mergers, acquisitions, or restructuring.

4. Data Security

We employ administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. However, no system is entirely secure.

5. Data Retention

Information is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill business and legal purposes, including compliance with KYC and AML requirements.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict processing, or withdraw consent for communications. To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@starlitecapitalpartners.com.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and analytics tools to enhance site performance. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function properly.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in the U.S., where data protection laws may differ from your local jurisdiction.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed toward individuals under 18, and we do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10. Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Updates will be posted here with a new "Last Updated" date.
